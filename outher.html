About the author

Dr B.R. patle

Born on 31st March 1938 in a farmer family in village Newargaon, T.Waraseoni Dt. Balaghat, MP. Dr. B.R.Patle served at various Veterinary Colleges of M.P. on various Scientific and Administrative posts and retired in the year 1998. He has published more than fifty research papers in the field of Animal Nutrition in various reputed Indian and Foreign research journals. In recognition to his research work he has been bestowed with three prestigious awards including the reputed Rafi Ahmed Kidwai Memorial Prize.
